Personal Project Site

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a place that allows me as an individual to advertise myself. To showcase my accomplishments and allow for easier communication and consolidation of everything that future employers might need, all in one place.

Audience

My audience will primarily be potential employers as well as anyone else who might need a list of my accomplishments and access to my resume.

Resume and Accolades

This will be the page of my site that will house and showcase, as the title describes, both my resume and any and all special awards, certificates, and achievements that I think would benefit me from an employers point of view. I will need to keep this page updated more than the other two because this will be the site that will go to highlight me and hopefully make me standout to others. It will be something small that will hopefully matter if it ever comes down to me and another person when being considered for a position at a company.
